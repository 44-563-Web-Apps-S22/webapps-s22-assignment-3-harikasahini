Zodiac grill Restaurant

The entire restaurant is decorated with Zodiac signs and the sitting arrangement was superb. Moreover, the best thing about the restaurant is its Multi-national cuisines.

Directions for Zodiac grill restaurant

  1. Hyderabad airport is closest airport to the Zodiac grill restaurant.
  2. Take a bus from airport to the Kukatpally main road.
  3. Get down at bus stop on main road.
    1. Walk for 1 mile to the right of the bus stop towards university junction.
    2. Cross the road and take exit 700.
    3. Walk straight towards MMR mall.
  4. The restaurant will be opposite to the MMR mall.

Recommended food items

  • Grilled Salamon dish
  • Mughalai Biryani
  • Banana Split Ice Cream
  • Manchow Soup
